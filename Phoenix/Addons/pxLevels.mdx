## Introduction

pxLevels is a fun and engaging plugin that adds a leveling system to your Minecraft network. Gain experience and level up to unlock rewards and enhance your gameplay experience.

## Commands and Permissions
| Command       | Permission              | Purpose                          |
| ------------- | ----------------------- | -------------------------------- |
| `/adminlevel` | core.command.adminlevel | Level management commands        |
| `/adminexp`   | core.command.adminexp   | Experience management commands   |
| `/level`      | -                       | Opens a level menu               |
| `/experience` | -                       | See how many experience you have |
