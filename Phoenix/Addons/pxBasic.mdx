## Commands

`<>` = Required `[]` = Optional

<!-- TODO: Check command parameters -->

Command                         | Permission                    | Description
------------------------------- | ----------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------
`/sudo <player> <chat command>` | `core.command.sudo`           | Talk or executes a command on behalf of another player.
`/masssay <chat command>`       | `core.command.masssay`        | Makes everyone say something or execute a command.
`/heal`                         | `core.command.heal`           | Heals a player to full health.
`/more`                         | `core.command.more`           | Gets a stack of the item you are currently holding.
`/skull <player>`               | `core.command.skull`          | Give yourself a player's head.
`/craft`                        | `core.command.craft`          | Opens the crafting table menu.
`/god`                          | `core.command.god`            | Toggles god mode.
`/staffalert <message>`         | `core.command.staffalert`     | Sends a cross server alert to all staff.
`/staffboardcast <message>`     | `core.command.staffbroadcast` | Sends a message to staff of your current server.
`/speed`                        | `core.command.speed`          | Changes your fly / walking speed.
`/broadcast <message>`          | `core.command.broadcast`      | Sends a message to everyone on your current server.
`/fly`                          | `core.command.fly`            | Toggles your flight.
`/feed`                         | `core.command.feed`           | Fills a players hunger bar to full.
`/clear`                        | `core.command.clear`          | Clears a players inventory.
`/enchant`                      | `core.command.enchant`        | Enchants the item you are currently holding.
`/gamemode <gamemode> [player]` | `core.command.gamemode`       | Changes a players gamemode.

`core.command.enderchest`       | Open your enderchest.
`core.command.shrug`            | Access to /shrug.
`core.command.item`             | Access to /item.
`core.command.kill`             | Access to /kill.
`core.command.addlore`          | Access to /addlore.
`core.command.removelore`       | Access to /removelore.
`core.command.rename`           | Access to /rename.
`core.command.pxbasic`          | Access to /pxbasic.
`core.command.repair`           | Access to /repair.
`core.command.repair.hand`      | Repair item in hand.
`core.command.repair.all`       | Repair all items.
`core.command.repair.armor`     | Repair armor.
`core.command.teleport`         | Access to /teleport.
`core.command.teleporthere`     | Access to /teleporthere.
`core.command.teleportall`      | Access to /teleportall.
`core.command.teleportposition` | Access to /teleportposition.
`core.command.teleportworld`    | Access to /teleportworld.
`core.command.top`              | Access to /top
